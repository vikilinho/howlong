Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

HowLong ("the App") is a personal time-tracking application that helps you monitor how long it has been since meaningful events and build or break habits. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely offline, and all data you create — events, habits, check-ins, and streaks — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device.

Specifically, the App does not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • Transmit any data over the internet
  • Use analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone

3. Biometric Authentication

The App offers an optional biometric lock (fingerprint or face recognition) to protect your data. This feature uses your device's built-in biometric framework. Your biometric data is never accessed by the App. Authentication is handled entirely by the operating system's secure enclave. The App only receives a pass/fail result.

4. Local Data Storage

All app data is persisted on-device using an embedded database. This data is not backed up to any cloud service by the App itself. Standard device backups (iCloud, Google Backup) may include app data depending on your device settings.

5. Notifications

The App may send local push notifications to remind you of habit check-ins. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device and do not require or use any external push notification service.

6. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can view your data, delete your data by uninstalling the app, or revoke permissions in your device settings.
