1 Introduction

HowLong ("the App") is a personal time-tracking application that helps you monitor how long it has been since meaningful events and build or break habits. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

2 Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely offline, and all data you create — events, habits, check-ins, and streaks — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device.

Specifically, the App does not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • Transmit any data over the internet
  • Use analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone

3 Biometric Authentication

The App offers an optional biometric lock (fingerprint or face recognition) to protect your data. This feature uses your device's built-in biometric framework (Android BiometricPrompt / iOS LocalAuthentication).

Your biometric data is never accessed by the App. Authentication is handled entirely by the operating system's secure enclave. The App only receives a pass/fail result — no fingerprint templates or facial data are ever stored or transmitted.

4 Local Data Storage

All app data is persisted on-device using an embedded database (Isar). This data includes:

  • Event names, descriptions, and timestamps
  • Habit names, frequencies, and check-in history
  • Streak and progress calculations
  • App preferences (theme, biometric lock toggle)

This data is not backed up to any cloud service by the App itself. Standard device backups (iCloud, Google Backup) may include app data depending on your device settings.

5 Notifications

The App may send local push notifications to remind you of habit check-ins. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device and do not require or use any external push notification service. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings.

6 Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data. There are no ads, no social logins, and no cloud syncing.

7 Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected from any user, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

8 Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • View your data — Open the App to see everything it stores
  • Delete your data — Uninstall the App or clear its storage in device settings to permanently erase all data
  • Revoke permissions — Disable biometric or notification access in your device settings at any time

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
