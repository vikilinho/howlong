HowLong 1.0.2

What is new:
- Added Daily Logs, a simple way to capture thoughts about your day alongside your actions and habits.
- Improved habit streak handling: broken streaks now clearly show Restart streak and begin again with a clean count.
- Expanded editing for actions and habits so names, reminders, notification timing, goals, schedules, and related details can be updated after creation.
- Improved daily log mood and energy chips with clearer color coding.
- Polished action and habit detail screens with clearer edit and delete controls.

Fixes and improvements:
- Fixed a widget lifecycle crash when closing edit dialogs.
- Improved reminder scheduling behavior after edits and streak restarts.
- Refined in-app copy and visual polish across tracking screens.
